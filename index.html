MEDITATION

Breath · Mindset · Focus · Recovery

🌅

Morning Mindfulness

5–10 min

Sit still after waking. Focus on breath, let thoughts pass. Sets the tone for your whole day.

🔥

Pre-Workout Visualization

3–5 min

Mentally rehearse every set with perfect form before touching a weight. Athletes who visualize perform up to 13% better.

🌊

Box Breathing

4–4–4–4 sec

Used by Navy SEALs to control stress instantly. Inhale → Hold → Exhale → Hold in equal counts.

🧠

Body Scan (Recovery)

10–15 min

Scan every muscle from head to toe, consciously releasing tension. Accelerates recovery and sleep quality.

🎯

Focus Meditation

10 min

Pick one object of attention. Every time the mind drifts, return. The returning IS the training.

🌙

Evening Wind-Down

5 min

4-count inhale, 8-count exhale. Activates the parasympathetic system for deep, restorative sleep.

🙏

Gratitude Practice

2–3 min

State 3 specific things you're grateful for. Linked to lower cortisol, better mood, and sustained motivation.
